## 2013 Design System

### Palette

- `--p13-bg`: warm off-white page background.
- `--p13-panel`: near-white card surface.
- `--p13-ink`: charcoal text.
- `--p13-muted`: soft gray copy.
- `--p13-line`: pale gray borders.
- `--p13-mint`: app-era mint accent.
- `--p13-coral`: coral action accent.
- `--p13-lime`: translucent lime headline strip.
- `--p13-navy`: charcoal/nav surface.
- `--p13-shadow`: soft card shadow.

No raw color values outside token definitions.

### Typography

- Inter for Latin/app chrome.
- Noto Sans KR for Korean body and headlines.
- Avoid serif masthead styling in this edition; 2013 references are app-like and sans-first.

### Material

- Base: off-white document page.
- Elevated: white cards with 1px borders and small shadow.
- Hero: real image backdrop with blur and desaturation, overlaid with colored title strips.
- Controls: squared or lightly rounded app-era buttons, search bars, tab-like links.
- Motion: small hover lifts only; no animated spectacle.

### Content Budget

- Home hero: one article, short subtitle, one CTA.
- Home grid: 9-12 articles.
- Article page: title, subtitle, image, body, heart, related/latest.

### Responsive Behavior

- Desktop: fixed central shell, two/three-column card grid.
- Tablet: two columns.
- Mobile: single column, compact hero title, app bar wraps cleanly.
